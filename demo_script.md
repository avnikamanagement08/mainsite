# Avanika.co Site Tour Demonstration Script

This script matches the flow of the recorded vertical video. It can be used as a **voiceover script** for an Instagram Reel/TikTok showcase or as a **step-by-step presentation guide** for stakeholders.

---

## 🎬 Part 1: Landing Page & Brand Identity (0:00 - 0:10)
* **Visuals**: Video starts. The custom logo loader fades out smoothly. Page loads, showcasing the luxury design system. Page scrolls down past the hero banner, category navigation pills, and the elegant curved announcement marquee.
* **Narration / Voiceover**:
  > *"Welcome to Avanika.co—a premium online store dedicated to high-quality, anti-tarnish artificial jewelry. Built with a sleek, mobile-first design, the site loads instantly with a custom brand preloader and guides users into a premium shopping experience."*

---

## 💛 Part 2: Wishlist Functionality (0:10 - 0:20)
* **Visuals**: The user scrolls back up, hovers over the first product card, and clicks the heart icon. A badge count increment is visible. The slide-out wishlist panel drawer opens from the right showing the added item, then closes.
* **Narration / Voiceover**:
  > *"Users can easily curate their favorites. With a single tap, items are added to a persistent wishlist drawer, allowing customers to save pieces they love without interrupting their browsing flow."*

---

## 🔍 Part 3: Product Quick View (0:20 - 0:28)
* **Visuals**: The user clicks on the first product card. A clean Quick View modal overlay slides up. Product details, specifications, and the 'Add to Cart' buttons are displayed (all pricing labels are hidden).
* **Narration / Voiceover**:
  > *"To keep the experience fast and frictionless, we implemented a Quick View modal. Customers can inspect product descriptions, check materials, and configure quantities without leaving the main page."*

---

## 🛒 Part 4: Add to Cart & Cart Drawer (0:28 - 0:35)
* **Visuals**: The user clicks 'Add to Cart' inside the modal. The modal closes, and the shopping cart drawer slides open, showcasing the item inside the cart list, along with a sticky CTA button to checkout.
* **Narration / Voiceover**:
  > *"Adding a piece to the cart automatically opens the side-drawer bag. This gives the shopper an immediate look at their cart contents and provides a direct, one-click path to checkout."*

---

## 💳 Part 5: Secure Checkout & Payment (0:35 - 0:45)
* **Visuals**: The checkout page loads.
  1. The user proceeds past Step 1 (Order Review).
  2. Step 2 (Shipping Address) opens, and shipping fields are filled out.
  3. Step 3 (Payment Method) opens, and the Cash on Delivery (COD) option is selected.
* **Narration / Voiceover**:
  > *"Our optimized checkout stepper simplifies the buying process. The buyer reviews their order, enters their shipping details, and chooses their preferred payment method—supporting both secure online payments and Cash on Delivery."*

---

## 🎉 Part 6: Order Success (0:45 - end)
* **Visuals**: The user clicks "Complete Order". The page transitions into the success page displaying a success checkmark badge and order confirmation.
* **Narration / Voiceover**:
  > *"Once confirmed, the order is placed instantly, directing the customer to a clean confirmation screen. A seamless, high-converting, and beautiful shopping experience from start to finish."*
